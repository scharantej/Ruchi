Lesson 1: Russian Alphabet and Pronunciation

In this lesson, you will learn the Russian alphabet and how to pronounce Russian letters and words.

  1. The Russian alphabet has 33 letters.
  2. The Russian alphabet is divided into vowels and consonants.
  3. Russian vowels are pronounced differently than English vowels.
  4. Russian consonants are pronounced differently than English consonants.
